FAQ

It’s a big universe. We have answers.

What is GitHub Universe?

Universe is GitHub’s flagship product and community conference for developers, system administrators, technical leads, and business leaders. Attendees will experience advanced skill building sessions, deep dives into how some of the most successful teams use GitHub, and keynotes from leading software company executives.

When is GitHub Universe?

  • Workshops – October 10, 2017 beginning at 8:00 am
  • Conference – October 11 & 12, 2017 beginning at 9:00 am
  • The After Party – October 12, 2017 (Doors open at 8:00 pm, Show starts at 9:00 pm)

How do I get to the venue?

You can get to the venue with public transportation (MUNI T-line), ridesharing services like Uber and Lyft, or bike with complimentary valet. There is no parking available at the venue and limited parking available in the neighborhood.

I'm from out of town. Where should I stay?

Airbnb has homes and rooms available in San Francisco that are sometimes less expensive than hotels. If you're planning to stay in a hotel, we recommend staying at one supported by fairhotel.org like:

  • Hyatt Regency San Francisco, 5 Embarcadero Center (Map)
  • Park Central Hotel, 50 Third Street (Map)
  • W San Francisco, 181 Third Street (Map)
  • Hotel Vitale, 8 Mission Street (Map)

Do you offer scholarships to GitHub Universe?

Yes, we offer free tickets to Universe via our Community Partners and Individual Scholarship programs. However, the deadline to participate in either of these programs has now passed.
